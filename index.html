Welcome to my webpage. I'm a doctoral student in the Sydney University Perception and Action Lab. My research examines visual working memory, the system responsible for online maintenance of visual information, which is essential for perception and cognition. I am interested in its limited capacity of approximately 3-4 items, and finding what factors influence this capacity limit, potentially increasing the amount of information available for visual processing.

You can find PDFs or any available open data of my publications and posters in the "Research" link on the top right of this page. You can also view my CV using the "CV" link or contact me using the "Email" link.

I also love to run, capture landscapes with my camera drone, and cheat on my vegetarianism.
