Graduate Student at the University of Sydney // Visual Working Memory // Reproducibility and Open Access in Science

Welcome to my webpage. I'm a doctoral student in the Sydney University Perception and Action Lab, supervised by Professor Alex Holcombe. My research examines the visual working memory system, which is responsible for online maintenance of visual information and is essential for perception and cognition. I mainly focus on the highly limited information capacity of this system, examining potential factors that allow this limit to be overcome and how that occurs.

You can find out more about me or my research using the links on the top right of this page.
